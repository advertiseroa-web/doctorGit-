# 🚀 doctorGit: The Raw AI Creation Canvas

> *"We have the architecture. Now let's build the Burj Khalifa."*

Welcome to **doctorGit**. We've stripped away the corporate jargon, the scientific names, and the heavy SaaS bloat. This is a pure creation space. You are here to *build* AI, not just talk to it.

## 🌟 How It Works
It all depends on your creativity. You have two choices:
1. **Start From Scratch:** A raw, blank canvas for your imagination. Build exactly what you want from the ground up, using your own patterns.
2. **Start From a Persona/Blueprint:** Jump right in with a fully configured system (like the Trump persona, or an expert engineer preset) and mould it to your specific needs.
3. **The 500 Patterns:** The engine already learns from the patterns and connections you throw into the vault. No slow web loops, just raw local execution.

## 🏗️ The Power Under the Hood (Ultra-Lightweight < 1 MB)
While the interface is beautifully simple and accessible, **doctorGit** is powered by a high-speed engine:
- **The Knowledge Vault (Dynamic Wiki):** Gathers knowledge actively. "What serve the user, learn the user, be the user." Your patterns live here.
- **Super Agents:** You are at the top. Sub-agents work beneath you to execute, break, fix, and route tasks instantly. No waiting.
- **Hidden Neural Network:** The heavy AI models are safely hidden in the AppBase. You only see the clean, blazing-fast, **< 1 MB** frontend application.
